Mekhi's Grilled Recipe

"How Mekhi makes his Grilled cheese"

  • Spray Pan
  • Grab cheese
  • Grab cooking spray
  • Grab 2 slices of bread
  • FLip sandwhich over
  • Turn stove burner on low
  • Form Sandwhich
  • Place pan on stove
  • Sprinkle crushed peppers on sandwhich
  • Cook unil cheese melts
  • Once sandwhich is tinted brown removes from pan

I like this grilled cheese because its a old fashion sandwhich with a little spice. I like to use spray instead of butter because to me it seems to have more a crunch. Spray also makes it more golden brown with a cruch. This sandwhich is only good if you make it right. Ive been eating my grilled cheeses this way since I was nine years old.
